Coffee

Coffee is one of the most commonly drunk beverages all over the word. The most surprising fact is that coffee has a diverse effect on us, ranging from social to economical advantages. Additionally, it is interesting to note that coffee can be extremely addictive if consumed on a daily basis.

With a cafe in almost every corner in many cities around the world, it is no suprise that the demand is high for them. Luckily there are many countries that export these beans. The top coffee exporting countries include:

  1. Brazil

  2. Vietnam

  3. Colombia
